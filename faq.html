FAQ



General questions

Is account registration required?

Account registration at ThyFlow is only required if you are checking out. This ensures a valid communication channel for all parties involved in any transactions.

Can I make order without downloading your Mobile App?

Yes, You can request for all thyflow services on your mobile without downloading the mobile app.

What is the currency used for all transactions?

All transactions on ThyFlow is based in USD
Haircut

What if I do not like my cut?

That has never happened, But we will take your request and refund your full payment.

How far in advance can I book an appointment ?

You can book an appointment 2 hours in advance.

About how long will each appointment last?

It varies from one hour to an Hour and a half. Depending on the haircut you choose.

How will quality be guaranteed?

All of our Barbers are professionals that take pride in their work. They take the time to ensure the quality and standard is met. Barbers are handpicked by industry professionals.

What are the payment options?

The best way to transfer funds is via Paypal. This secure platform ensures timely payments and a secure environment.

Do I need to provide anything?

Yes, An area for the haircut to take place, Like your backyard, garage, or an empty room, You can use your office chair. Barbers will have own chair if you don't have a good standard chair.
Barbers

How experienced are the barbers?

ThyFlow Barbers have a minimum of 3+ years of experience cutting hair, Before joining the platform.

Can I guarantee I will get the same barber each time?

Depending on the area you live, Most of our barbers service their neighborhood and local areas, But if you rate a barber below 3 stars, you will not get the same barber again.
